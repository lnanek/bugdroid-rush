Bugdroid Rush!

Story

Oh, no! The sign for Google I/O has broken into scattered pieces! Help Bugdroid collect them in proper order as quick as you can. All the excited attendees are relying on you to make the best conference possible!

Instructions

Move your mouse in the box with Bugdroid to each part of the scattered Google I/O logo in order. Move as fast as you can, but be careful! Hitting the wrong piece, walls, or attendees will scatter the pieces again. Try for the best time!
